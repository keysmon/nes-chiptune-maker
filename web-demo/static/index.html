Chiptune Maker - live playground

A song separated, transcribed, and re-arranged for the NES 2A03's four voices - then tuned live in your browser. This hosted demo ships pre-analyzed songs and runs the synthesizer per request. To convert your own songs, run the full app locally: github.com/keysmon/nes-chiptune-maker.
